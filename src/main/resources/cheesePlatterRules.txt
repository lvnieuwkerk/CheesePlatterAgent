1. The platter will only contain cheeses of the five listed cheese families below:
- Hard and semi-hard cheese
- White mold cheese
- Red and white rind cheese
- Goat and sheep cheese
- Blue cheese
2. There can only be one cheese of every cheese family on the platter.
3. It should contain at least one mild cheese, one semi-strong cheese and one strong cheese but should always have a total of five cheeses.
4. All suggested cheeses have to be produced in the Netherlands by local producers.
